What is GTOsint?

GTOsint, short for Gaming Tracker Open Source INtelligence, is a platform dedicated to OSINT applied to video games. Unlike general-purpose tools, GTOsint allows you to link online gamer profiles.


OSINT Applied to Gaming


OSINT (Open Source Intelligence) involves searching and analyzing publicly available data that can be accessed legally. Many gamers use the same usernames or addresses across different platforms. GTOsint helps cross-reference this information to trace their digital footprints.


Why Use GTOsint?


Whether you are a gamer, a cybersecurity analyst, or simply curious, GTOsint provides precise tools tailored to the gaming world. This open-source project is designed to integrate with other existing OSINT tools.
